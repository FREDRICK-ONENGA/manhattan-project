Dive in

Across the space, my eyes take flight,
A silent language, day and night.
Your smile, a sunbeam, warm and bright,
Across the room, you hold my light.
My heart, a drum, begins to beat,
A rhythm sweet, both strong and neat.
Each glance a promise, bittersweet,
Across the room, our worlds meet.
Though words may fail, my feelings bloom,
Dispelling doubt, and chasing gloom.
Across the room, I long to come,
And whisper, "You're my only one."
