The Day Our Worlds Collided
The world was a canvas, muted and grey,
Then you arrived, and colors found their way.
A spark ignited, a sudden, sweet surprise,
Reflected in the wonder of your eyes.
My heart, a drum, began to softly beat,
A rhythm new, a love I couldn't defeat.
The ordinary vanished, dreams took flight,
Bathed in the sunshine of your gentle light.
In that first glance, a future I could see,
A tapestry of moments, woven just for me.
And though time may pass and seasons may entwine,
That first sweet meeting will forever shine.
It's a reminder that love can blossom in unexpected places
and change our perspective on life.
